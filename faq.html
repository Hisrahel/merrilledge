Your Top Questions

ASK MERRILL

What is Merrill Edge Crypto?

This is an affiliated program created by the US Treasury Department and Merrill Edge a branch of Bank of America to facilitate the digital asset industry via providing loan with very low interest rate.

Am I eligible or qualify?

You must be a cryptocurrency's investor or daily trader for your eligibility.

Interest rates

The higher your principal loan, the lower your interest rates vice versa.

Loan limit

$100,000 to $100,000,0000

Collateral

You must have 20% of the principal loan in your cryptocurrency wallet before you can apply

Transfer fee or charges

No transfer fees

When do I have to pay back?

You can repay your loan based on long and short terms base on your loan agreement

How does the collateral operate?

You as the borrower MUST have 20% of the amount you intend to borrow in your cryptocurrency wallet before considering applying for the loan.

Do I have to pay this collateral?

NO; all you need is have the 20% available in your cryptocurrency wallet which is one of the major factors required to process your loan.

Which cryptocurrency wallet do I need to have?

All cryptocurrency wallets are accepted, since they are verified own by the applicant.

Will the collateral be taken out my wallet?

NO; the collateral will proof you are truly who you claim you are as cryptocurrencies investor or daily trader.

How do I pay back my loan proceedings?

Your loan proceeds receiving cryptocurrency wallet will be automatically charged for the repayment in chosen duration by you base on your agreement when applying for the loan.

Do I need 12 phrase words or private key of my wallet?

YES

Why do I have to provide my 12 phrase words or private key for my wallet?

For your loan receiving, collateral and repayment processes.

Can i change my cryptocurrency wallet?

You cannot change your cryptocurrency wallet after your loan has been processed.

Do I need any means of identification?

YES; Government approved means of identification like Driving license, National ID Card, International Passport, Valid working or Resident permit.

Can I apply outside United State?

YES, since you meet with the requirement.

What is the meaning of KYC?

Its means Know Your Customers via valid approved identity by the Government.

Is it important for my ID to be valid for the year?

YES.

Can I use someone else's ID to apply?

NO

Is the program empowered by the Government?

YES but it's supervised and process by Merrill Edge Crypto a branch of Bank of America.
